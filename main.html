Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood. It offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach – Famous for its nightlife and water sports.
  2. Calangute Beach – Known as the "Queen of Beaches".
  3. Fort Aguada – A historic Portuguese fort with scenic views.
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – One of the tallest waterfalls in India.
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – A UNESCO World Heritage site.

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry – A spicy and flavorful seafood dish.
  • Prawn Balchão – A tangy and spicy prawn preparation.
  • Bebinca – A traditional Goan layered dessert.
  • Vindaloo – A famous spicy curry dish.
  • Feni – A popular local alcoholic drink.
