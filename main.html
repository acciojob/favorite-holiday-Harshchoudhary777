Goa


Goa is a beautiful holiday destination in India known for its beaches, nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood.

  1. Baga Beach is famous for nightlife.
  2. Calangute Beach is known as the Queen of Beaches.
  3. Fort Aguada offers scenic views.
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls is a major attraction.
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus is a historic site.
  • Goan Fish Curry is very popular.
  • Prawn Balchão is a spicy dish.
  • Bebinca is a traditional dessert.
  • Vindaloo is a famous curry.
  • Feni is a local drink.
